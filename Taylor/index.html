Is TTPD the Saddest Album of Taylor Swift?

By Manyun Zou

I bet you can also feel the heartbreaking vibe from Taylor's latest album, The Tortued Poets Department (TTPD). And you might also ponder over the same question as mine: is this her saddest album?

Some of TTPD's songs have definitely made it to the ranking of Taylor's saddest songs. After all, she released this album after the breakup of a 6-year relationship. But surprisingly, according to computer judgment, which can be vastly different from human feelings, the saddest song overall comes from another popular album, 1989.

Here is the chart. As you can see, on the left end, which represents the most negative emotion scores, four songs come from 1989.

Taylor Swift's Songs by Emotion

The top 10 saddest songs are mostly from three albums, 1989, TTPD, and Lover:

Shake It Off, This Love, I Wish You Would, I Knew You Were Trouble, Down Bad, Cassandra, Bad Blood, The Albatross, Miss Americana & The Heartbreak Prince, and I Forgot That You Existed.

What is more surprising is that Taylor's saddest album, judged by the computer, is none of these three. The chart below presents 11 studio albums released by Swift since 2006. The longer the bar is, the higher positive scores the album has. (Yes, overall, all Taylor's albums are positive.)

And the answer is: Folklore is the saddest album, and TTPD comes the next.
