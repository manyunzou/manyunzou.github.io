Greetings, this is Manyun Zou. You can call me Mandy. I am a data reporter based in Shanghai.

For the very special year of 2020, I have been written about issues concerning COVID-19, like epidemiological data, vaccines, and business relief policies.

It was a dark year, but it pushed me to reflect on where I am heading as a data reporter. Therefore, I visualized all the stories I pitched, researched, analyzed, and wrote for the past three years, trying to figure out topics I cared about and my improvements in coding and design skills.

As it shows on the right side, business, technology, gender, and government policies are the topics I wrote most about. Of course, I am not always delivering hard stories. The other topics include culture, psychology, and entertainment, and more. I also put up a side project website for my trivial story ideas.

One of the biggest progress I made this year is that I finally get to apply coding skills to my stories regularly, either for data analysis or visualization, which let me have more time to ponder over the content.
